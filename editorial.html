PICT DEBSOC is delighted to announce the release of the very first edition of its editorial. This editorial consists of carefully curated articles on current issues that have been written by the club's members after carrying out in-depth research. Each article presents a case for the topic as well as against the topic, thus ensuring that readers are exposed to a broad spectrum of perspectives. We hope that this editorial reaches a large audience and engages our readers.
